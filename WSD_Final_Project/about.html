Our Story

We build brands that feel lived-in and lasting.

Terracotta Studio began as a two-person collaboration and has grown into a multidisciplinary team that ships warm, human experiences for clients who care about detail.

Our principles

  • Accessibility is non-negotiable.
  • Research before pixels.
  • Mobile-first, content-first.
  • Transparent, weekly updates.
Strategy & Discovery

Workshops to define audience, tone, and success metrics before design begins.

Design Systems

Component libraries and tokens that keep your brand consistent across channels.

Content & Copy

Messaging frameworks that keep every line on-brand, from headlines to CTAs.

Launch & Care

QA, accessibility sweeps, and post-launch optimization to keep things running smoothly.
