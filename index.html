Death Becomes Her

Director: Robert Zemeckis

Genre: Dark Fantasy Comedy

Year Released: 1992

Awards: Academy Award for Best Visual Effects
Fun Fact: The film was one of the first to use extensive CGI for character effects

Death Becomes Her is a 1992 American dark fantasy comedy film that tells the story of two rival women, Madeline Ashton and Helen Sharp, who discover a magical potion that grants eternal youth.
However, this immortality comes with unexpected consequences. The film stars Meryl Streep, Goldie Hawn, and Bruce Willis, and was directed by Robert Zemeckis.
The movie explores themes of vanity, immortality, and the price of eternal beauty through dark humor and groundbreaking visual effects.
